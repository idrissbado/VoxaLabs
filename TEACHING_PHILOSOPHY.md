## What Real Teachers Do

A good math teacher:

1. **Listens to the problem** - Understands exactly what you're asking
2. **Assesses your level** - "Are you beginner or advanced?"
3. **Asks guiding questions** - Doesn't give answers, guides thinking
4. **Validates reasoning** - Checks both algebra AND logic
5. **Explains mistakes** - "Here's where you went wrong and why"
6. **Reinforces learning** - Reviews similar concepts
7. **Builds confidence** - Celebrates progress, encourages struggle

```

```

### Level 5 Problems (Advanced)
```

Because advanced students need intellectual challenge.
```
